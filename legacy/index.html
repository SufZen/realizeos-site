Foundations

A

B

Domain knowledge your team actually remembers. Market data, expertise, research — accessible every conversation.

R

Workflows that chain agents and tools together. "Write a post" triggers writer, then reviewer — automatically.

I

C

Built for Real Operations

Not a toy. Not a wrapper. A complete AI operations engine with the depth to run your business.

Multi-LLM Routing

Each request routed to the optimal model. Quick questions go to Gemini Flash. Content to Claude Sonnet. Complex strategy to Claude Opus. Automatic.

7-Layer Prompt Assembly

Every response built from your identity, brand voice, agent role, relevant knowledge, memory, and session context. Not a generic chatbot — your chatbot.

Multi-Step Skills

YAML-defined workflows that chain agents and tools: search the web, analyze results, draft content, get review — all triggered by one message.

Hybrid KB Search

FTS5 keyword search combined with vector embeddings. Your agents find the right knowledge every time — not just keywords, but meaning.

Creative Pipelines

Multi-agent sessions with stages: briefing, drafting, iterating, reviewing, approved. Your content goes through a real production flow.

Self-Evolution

The system tracks what works and what doesn't. Detects gaps, suggests new skills, refines agent prompts — getting better every interaction.
