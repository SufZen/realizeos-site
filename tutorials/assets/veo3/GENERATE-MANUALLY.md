## Assembly Options

**Option A — Flow Scene Builder:**
Drag all clips into Scene Builder within your Flow project. Use "Jump To" for transitions. Export as single videos.

**Option B — Descript:**
Download all clips individually. In Descript, stitch 2a+2b into one 16-sec clip and 3a–3f into one 48-sec clip. Overlay correct text in Descript if any labels come out garbled.

## Troubleshooting

- **Text/labels garbled?** AI video generators struggle with text. Plan to overlay correct text in Descript or your editor.
- **Style drift between clips?** Make sure you're using the same Ingredients AND the saved Start Frame for each continuation clip.
- **Hexagons look different between clips?** Reinforce in your prompt: "identical golden hexagonal tile with a thin gold border" — and always use the Gold Hexagon ingredient.
- **Background color shifts?** Always include "Deep navy-black background, hex #0e0e18" in every prompt.
