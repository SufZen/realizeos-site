# Descript Text Overlay Specs

These 4 cards failed AI image generation (Imagen 3 can't reliably render precise text).
Create them as text overlays in Descript during video editing.

## Design System

- **Background**: #0e0e18 (solid, full frame)
- **Primary accent**: #ffcc00 (gold)
- **Title font**: Poppins Bold, white
- **Subtitle font**: Poppins Regular, white or #cccccc
- **Code font**: JetBrains Mono, white
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (16:9)
